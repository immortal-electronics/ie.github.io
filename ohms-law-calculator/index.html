Overview

Ohm’s law deals with the connection between Voltage, current, and Resistance. It is the most important and almost first learned concept for the Electronics and Electrical Engineers. This law was developed by Georg Ohm in 1827.

This tool will assist you in calculating any two of voltage, current, obstruction or power, on the off chance that you have to provide two parameters among them. You simply enter any two parameters among the four and click “Calculate”, we will show to you the remaining two parameters.

Equations

V = IR

P = VI

Where:

V = voltage

I = current

R = resistance

P = power
