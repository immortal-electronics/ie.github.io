Applications

A capacitor is an electronic component which can hold the charge, the amount of charge stored by a capacitor can be referred to as Capacitance. From the above formula, we can observe that capacitance is directly proportional to charge. The capacitor is one of the three basic circuit components along with Resistor and Inductor. It is used in applications where energy is needed to be stored and even in filters and oscillators.
